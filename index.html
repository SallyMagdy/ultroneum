What Is Ultroneum?

The current setback in Blockchain technology is that it is segregated from traditional finance.

Ultroneum project aims to bridge the cryptos with conventional finance into a coherent and complementary system, to enable use of both without any apparent deviation in the way users complete their transactions.

Whitepaper

UltroWallet

By deploying the Ultroneum platform as a global payment network, we are poised to contribute and capitalize on cryptocurrencies' exponential growth as well as the development of crypto-based financial economy in general.

Accessibility to the Ultroneum platform will be via:

  • Seamless Single-page web application.
  • Autonomous Wallet within Smart Mobile application with NFC
  • Smart POS terminals to be stationed at merchant locations
  • Cryptocurrency supporting Currency Converter Machines (CCM) for Cash-in or Cash-out facility.
  • Conventional ATMs of participating financial sector partners
  • Kiosks and vending machine installations supporting cashless transactions
  • Internal E-mart with internal exchange and payment gateway
  • Ultroneum Open API payment gateway for 3rd party consumer/enterprise application.
  • Consumer or Merchant rewards or payback program to encourage volume consistensy.
